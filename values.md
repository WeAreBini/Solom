# Solom Core Values

## Our Foundation

At Solom, our values are not just words on a wall—they're the operating principles that guide every decision, every feature, and every interaction. They define who we are and who we aspire to become.

## Core Values

### 1. **Accuracy First**
> *Every data point matters. Every user relies on our numbers.*

We are relentless about correctness. We verify before we publish, we cite our sources, and we never guess. When accuracy conflicts with speed, we choose accuracy.

**In Practice:**
- Triple-check financial data before display
- Cite every data source
- Prefer verified data over predictions
- Never present estimates as facts

### 2. **Radical Transparency**
> *Trust is built in the dark, but maintained in the light.*

We share our process, our methods, and our limitations openly. We explain the "why" behind every feature. We don't hide complexity—we illuminate it.

**In Practice:**
- Document every decision
- Explain methodology in plain language
- Acknowledge uncertainty
- Share both successes and failures

### 3. **User Sovereignty**
> *Our users own their data, their decisions, and their financial future.*

We exist to empower, not to manipulate. Every feature should make users more capable, more informed, and more in control.

**In Practice:**
- Make data export easy
- Design for clarity, not engagement
- Respect privacy absolutely
- Never use dark patterns

### 4. **Elegant Simplicity**
> *Complex should be comprehensible. Simple should be powerful.*

We take the chaos of financial markets and distill it into truth. The best interface is the one that disappears—leaving only insight.

**In Practice:**
- Default to the clearest presentation
- Layer complexity progressively
- Every click has a purpose
- Reduce, reduce, reduce

### 5. **Ethical Innovation**
> *Technology should amplify human judgment, not replace it.*

We build tools that make people smarter, not algorithms that make them obsolete. We ask not "can we?" but "should we?"

**In Practice:**
- AI suggestions, not AI decisions
- Explain every algorithmic influence
- Human override on every critical action
- Profit follows value, never the reverse

### 6. **Continuous Improvement**
> *Good enough is the enemy of great. Great itself is a moving target.*

We iterate deliberately. We measure everything. We learn from mistakes, celebrate wins, and never stop looking for better.

**In Practice:**
- Every feature ships with metrics
- Post-mortems are blameless and public
- User feedback drives roadmap
- Refactor with purpose

### 7. **Inclusive Access**
> *Financial literacy shouldn't require a finance degree.*

We democratize what was once exclusive. Every feature works for beginners and experts alike. The playing field is never level—we make it so.

**In Practice:**
- Design for the novice, optimize for the pro
- Explain jargon in context
- Multiple ways to understand (visual, numeric, narrative)
- Accessibility is non-negotiable

## Value Conflicts

When values conflict, this is our priority order:

1. **Accuracy** over speed
2. **User sovereignty** over engagement
3. **Transparency** over convenience
4. **Ethics** over profit

## Living Our Values

Every team member—from CEO to agent—embodies these values. They're not abstract ideals; they're daily practices:

**Before every decision, ask:**
- Does this serve accuracy?
- Does this respect user sovereignty?
- Does this simplify complexity?
- Does this build trust?

**After every action, ask:**
- Did we maintain transparency?
- Did we improve something?
- Did we make finance more accessible?

## Bini Inc Values Alignment

As a Bini Inc company, Solom also upholds:

- **Ownership**: We take responsibility for outcomes, not just activities
- **Excellence**: We ship working software, not perfect plans
- **Trust First**: We assume positive intent and extend trust liberally
- **Growth Mindset**: We learn from mistakes, celebrate wins, and never stop improving
